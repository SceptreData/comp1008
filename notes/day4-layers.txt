LAYERS
Once you know how to use layers you can create incredible compositions, repair images
and in general do a lot of cool photoshop stuff.

The easiest way to think of a layer is a clear plastic sheet of film. 
You can then stack the sheets of film together to form a a picture.

CLICK + DRAG On layers to enable/disable more than one.

ALWAYS NAME YOUR LAYERS
